Before you start

You must send your application to us before your normal filing deadline. You can apply for more time to file if something has happened that is out of your control and you cannot file your company accounts on time. It should include a full explanation of why you need the extension.

We will not issue you a late filing penalty if your application is approved and you file your accounts before the extended deadline.
